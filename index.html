How does this work?

We take unstructured home inspection reports and generate amazing online reports.

  • 1

    Send us the inspection report

    Whether you're a home buyer needing help understanding the home inspection, or a realtor who wants to give your customers an amazing service - we're here to help! Send us the inspection report by email, upload or dropbox. PDF, Word, printed or hand written. 10 pages or 200 pages, printed or hand written - as long as it's readable, we'll take it!

  • 2

    Our experts are hard at work

    Our team of experts read, analyze and digitize the report. We go over every tiny detail so that you won't have to, making sure everything is in place. We then feed all the details into our compute systems.

  • 3

    Awesome Report - Ready!

    Our AI systems generate a detailed, personalized report, highlighting the items that are important to you and their quoted costs. Get a clear high-level summary of problems and concerns, or drill down to specific details. Easy access to the total reported costs, and comparison with similar properties in the area.

  • 4

    Tons of Benefits

    There are lots of benefits from having the inspection report available online. Buyers can comment and discuss concerns directly on the report. Professionals compete on beating the corrections and renovatons quotes in the report. Insurers give lower rates based on the condition of the property.
