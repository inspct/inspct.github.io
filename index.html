How does this work?

We take unstructured home inspection reports and generate amazing online reports.

  • .1

    Send us the inspection report

    Whether you're a home buyer needing help understanding the home inspection, or a realtor who wants to give your customers an amazing service - we're here to help! Send us the inspection report by email, upload or dropbox. PDF, Word, printed or hand written. 10 pages or 200 pages, printed or hand written - as long as it's readable, we'll take it!

  • 2.

    Our experts are hard at work

    Our team of experts read, analyze and digitize the report. We go over every tiny detail so that you won't have to, making sure everything is in place. We then feed all the details into our compute systems.

  • .3

    Awesome Report - Ready!

    Our AI systems generate a detailed, personalized report, highlighting the items that are important to you and their quoted costs. Get a clear high-level summary of problems and concerns, or drill down to specific details. Easy access to the total reported costs, and comparison with similar properties in the area.

  • 4.

    Competing Professionals

    Vendors and suppliers will compete on beating the quotes you see in the report. We have plumbers, roofers, electricians, contractors and more professionals - all lined up to give you excellent rennovation quotes.

Who?

Everyone benefits from a comprehensible inspection report.

Home Buyers

Got a report you need to analyze? Send it to us and we'll give you the gist, the important issues that matter most, what you need to consider before buying and a breakdown of the costs. All in one online report, searchable with all the highlights clearly marked. In addition, you can receive competing offers on the costs listed in the report, giving you the best deals without any effort.

Realtors

Whether you are the listing agent, or the buyer's agent, you can add value to your customer by having a perfectly clear home inspection report. Moreover, you get full analytics of the potential buyers that accessed the report, and the ability communicate with them in real-time regarding concerns they might be having as they go over the report in the website.
