What?

We modernize home inspection reports.

Digital Inspection Report

Inspection reports are an essential part of the home buying process. We give them searchable structure and take them online. The home buyer can finally gain access to a clear and detailed inspection report, branded for you, the owner of the report.

Communicate

The online report opens a communication channel between report owners (listing agents, home inspectors) and potential buyers, where buyers can point out concerns and agents can address these concerns in real-time.

Customer Analytics

Forget about counting disclosure packets as a proxy for interest. Get all the details about who opened the report, when, and what was important to them. Interact with them in real-time, and see who is really interested in the property.

Renovation Quotes

Get competing improvement and renovation offers from top professionals in your area. Get better insurance rates. Letting professionals gain access to the report improves your brand and service you provide to your customers.

How does this work?

We take unstructured home inspection reports and generate amazing online reports.

  • 1

    Send us the inspection report

    Whether you're a realtor who wants to give your customers an amazing service or a home inspector that wants to take the extra step - we're here to help! Send us the inspection report by email, upload or dropbox. PDF, Word, printed or hand written. 10 pages or 200 pages, printed or hand written - as long as it's readable, we'll take it!

  • 2

    Our experts are hard at work

    Our team of experts read, analyze and digitize the report. We go over every tiny detail so that you won't have to, making sure everything is in place. We then feed all the details into our compute systems.

  • 3

    Awesome Report - Ready!

    Our AI and machine learning systems generate a detailed, personalized report, branded with the owner of the report (realtor, home inspector), highlighting the items that are important to you and their quoted costs. Get a clear high-level summary of problems and concerns, or drill down to specific details. Easy access to the total reported costs, and comparison with similar properties in the area.

  • 4

    Tons of Benefits

    There are lots of benefits from having the inspection report available online. Buyers can comment and discuss concerns directly on the report, with realtors and home inspectors leveraging their expertise to help their customers. Professionals compete on beating the corrections and renovatons quotes in the report. Insurers give lower rates based on the condition of the property.

Who?

Everyone benefits from a comprehensible inspection report.

Realtors

Whether you are the listing agent, or the buyer's agent, you can add value to your customer by having a clear, structured home inspection report.Moreover, you get full analytics of the potential buyers that accessed the report, and the ability communicate with them in real-time regarding concerns they might be having as they go over the report in the website.

Home Inspectors

Differentiate yourself for competitors by offering your inspection report online in a whole new way. In addition, once you use the service, you'll get a chance to propose competing quotes for your services in other inspection reports and engage with customers to show off your expertise.

Home Buyers

Are you looking at buying a home and need some help with understanding the inspection report? Send it to us and we'll give you a summary of what matters most, what you need to consider before buying and a breakdown of costs.All in one online report, searchable with all the highlights clearly marked. If you want to, we'll even get you in touch with professionals that can give you competing offers on the costs listed in the report, giving you the best deals with zero effort.
